PEBL Aimed Movement Task: a paradigm to measure Fitts's Law using mouse movement.  Quickly move cursor from home position to target, which has varying sizes and distances from home.
Data are saved in fitts-X.txt and fittspoints-X.txt in the fitts directory.
To cite: Mueller, S. T. (2011). The PEBL Aimed Movement Task.  Computer software retrieved from http://pebl.sf.net/battery.html
