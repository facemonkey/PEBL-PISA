PEBL Attention Network Test (PANT): a paradigm to assess three types of attentional networks.  An implementation of the ANT test by Fan et al. (2002).
Data are saved to ANT\data\ANT-X.csv and ANT\data/ANT-summary-X.txt.

To cite:  Mueller, S. T. (2011). The PEBL Attentional Network Test (PANT). Computer software retrieved from http://pebl.sf.net/battery.html
