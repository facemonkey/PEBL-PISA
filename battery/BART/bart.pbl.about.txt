PEBL Balloon Analog Risk Task: a paradigm to assess risk-seeking behavior that balances gains with increasing risk. Blow up a ballon but collect the money before it pops.
Data are saved to bart-X.csv in the BART directory.

To cite:  Mueller, S. T. (2011). The PEBL Balloon Analog Risk Task (PBART).  Computer software retrieved from http://pebl.sf.net/battery.html
